2 — GViz Cache-Bust Monitor
Polls VoteAPI.fetchGvizSheet() on the same cadence as screen.html and logs the row count + a content fingerprint at each tick. Run this during or right after a burst above — if the row count or fingerprint changes within a couple of poll cycles, the cache-buster (&_cb=Date.now()) is doing its job and Hazard 3 is closed. If it stays flat for 30–60s while you know votes were sent, that's the Sheets-side cache reasserting itself and is worth re-testing against the live sheet, not just this harness.
